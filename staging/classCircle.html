Gedetailleerde Beschrijving

Represents a circle and extends the Geometry class.

This class is used to represent a circle. It holds the radius of the circle along with its position in the form of a Vector2D inherited from the Geometry class. It implements the draw method to provide the logic for rendering the circle.
