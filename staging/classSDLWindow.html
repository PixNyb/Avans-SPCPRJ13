Gedetailleerde Beschrijving

Manages creation, display, and destruction of an SDL_Window.

This class wraps the SDL_Window structure and provides methods to create and destroy a window. It is responsible for handling the window's lifecycle and providing a means to retrieve the SDL_Window pointer.
